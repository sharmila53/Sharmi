KNOW & GROW YOUR TEAMMATE

Introduction

Team Presentation-This type of activity is commonly used for team bonding and communication enhancement,understanding strengths, interests, and backgrounds of your teammates.

How It Might Have Happened

1. Pairing Up or Team Formation:   

  •     You were probably divided into small teams or pairs.  
  •     Each person had to interview their teammate and learn about them.  

2. Information Gathering: 

   You might have asked your teammates questions like:  

  •    What is your name and background?  
  •    What are your interests and skills?
  •    Why did you join Forge?
  •   What are your future career goals?
  •    Fun facts or hobbies?

3. Presenting the Introduction: 

  •    After gathering information, each person introduced their teammate to the group instead of introducing themselves.  
  •    This encouraged active listening and ensured that everyone learned about each other.  

4. Outcome and Benefits:  

  •    Built a sense of teamwork and collaboration.  
  •    Helped in improving communication skills.  
  •    Allowed you to understand your teammates better, which can be useful for future teamwork.
