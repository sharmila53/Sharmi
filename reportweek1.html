5S CONCEPT:

  1. Sort:  Get rid of anything that's unnecessary. Keep only what you really need to do the job.

  3.  Shine:  Clean the workplace regularly. A clean space is safer and more efficient to work in.
  4. Standardize:  Create standard ways to do things. This helps keep the workspace organized and clean consistently.
  5. Sustain:  Continuously ensure high quality by monitoring processes, finding improvements, and making changes to maintain consistent excellence.
